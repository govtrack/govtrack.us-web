Backer Rewards!

Thanks so much for financially supporting our work!! If you’ve been given this link, then we owe you some free merch for your support.

Here’s how you can get your reward:

  1. Scroll down to find the item you want: a short or long sleeve t-shirt, tote bag, mouse pad, or desk mat.
  2. Click the Order ... button underneath the item you want.
  3. Enter your shipping address on our fulfillment partner website Printful.
    Please use the same email address you use on Kickstarter/Patreon, if you can.
    You won’t be asked for billing information.
  4. Please give us a week to confirm your order. Shipments should go out about 2 weeks after you place the order.
  5. If we owe you two items, you can do this a second time. You can’t combine different products into the same order — just place another order.
